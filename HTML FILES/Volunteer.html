Become a Volunteer in Our Shelter

Volunteers are the heart of our rescue work. By giving your time, you help us feed, care for, and comfort abandoned animals that seek refuge with us. From walking dogs and cleaning kennels to helping with events, every effort you make makes a big difference.

Volunteer Tasks

  • Feeding and grooming animals
  • Walking and playing with dogs
  • Cleaning cages and kennels
  • Assisting with adoptions and events
  • Sharing love and attention

Call to Action

If you are interested in volunteering or joining our family, please fill in the volunteer form. Together we can give every animal a second chance.

Donations

Support our rescue

Your donations help us provide food, shelter, and medical care for abandoned and rescued animals. Every contribution counts.

How Your Donations Help

  • R100 feeds a puppy for a week
  • R250 provides vaccines for one dog or cat
  • R500 covers medical care for a rescued animal
  • R1000 helps us with shelter repairs and supplies

You can also donate kennels, toys, food, blankets, clothes and more. Anything to ensure our animals are cared for and comfortable.
