About Our Organisation

Per year in South Africa we get over 20,000 cases of abandoned, hurt and unhealthy animals. Helping Hand Rescue Animals South Africa was founded by Athol Fenderick in October 2009 to help animals in need of shelter, nutrition and love. The main purpose of Helping Hands SA is to improve the lives of abused and abandoned animals. With the help of a great support committee and followers, we have been able to raise funds through events such as picnics where you bring along your furry best friend and connect with others. We are firm believers and supporters of no-kill animal shelters and through our efforts we raise awareness of the cruelty and torture some animals go through.

"The real heroes are the animals that have been hurt but continue to love us unconditionally."

Fun Facts About Our Rescued Friends

  • Every rescued animal is given a name that matches its personality.
  • 3 out of 5 volunteers secretly admit they have a “favorite rescue” they can’t stop visiting.
  • A rabbit and a stray dog became inseparable after being rescued on the same day.
