Welcome to Helping Hands

About Our Organisation

Helping Hands Rescue Animal South Africa is a dedicated nonprofit committed to giving abandoned, injured, and neglected animals a second chance at life. Since our founding in 2009 by Athol Fenderick, we have focused on rescuing animals in need, providing medical care, shelter, and nourishment, and finding them loving forever homes. Through community engagement, volunteer support, and educational initiatives, we aim to raise awareness about animal welfare, promote responsible pet ownership, and create a safer, more compassionate environment for all animals.

Join the conversation on all social media platforms about changing the lives of our pets and best friends.

Quick Statistic

87% of animals rescued by Helping Hands find their “furever” homes within the first 6 months of rescue!
